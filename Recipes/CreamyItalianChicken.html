Creamy Italian Chicken

Suggested with love by Mindy, Tom, Caroline, Charlotte, Molly, and Lucy!

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Breasts, boneless & skinless
  • 1 pkt of salad dressing mix (Italian or Marsala)
  • ¼ C water
  • 1 8 oz pkg cream cheese
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 4 oz can of mushrooms and stem pieces
  • Cooked noodles
  • Fried Onion Rings

Directions

  1. Combine water and salad dressing. Mix in slow cooker. Add chicken
  2. Cook for 3 hours on low
  3. Beat cream cheese and soup until blended in a small bowl. Stir in mushrooms. Pour over chicken. 
  4. Continue cooking for 1 more hour.
  5. Pour over prepared noodles. Sprinkle onion rings over the top
