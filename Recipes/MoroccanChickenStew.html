Slow Cooker Moroccan Chicken Stew

Suggested with love by Grandma Betty and Grandpa Steve!

Ingredients

  • 2 T tomato paste
  • 1 T vegetable oil
  • 1 T paprika
  • 1 t garam masala
  • 1 ½ C chicken broth
  • 1 14-oz can chickpeas, rinsed
  • ¼ C dry white wine
  • 1 T instant tapioca
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs, trimmed
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 T chopped dried apricots

Directions

  1. Lightly spray inside of slow cooker with vegetable oil spray.
  2. Microwave tomato paste, oil, paprika, and garam masala in bowl stirring occasionally, until fragrant, about 1 min; transfer to prepared slow cooker.
  3. Stir in broth, chickpeas, wine, and tapioca.
  4. Season chicken with salt and pepper and nestle into slow cooker. Cover and cook until chicken tender, 3 to 4 hours on low.
  5. Using large spoon, skim excess fat from surface of stew.
  6. Break chicken into 1” pieces with tongs.
  7. Stir in apricots and let sit until heated through, about 5 mins. Adjust stew consistency with extra broth as needed.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve on couscous or white rice
