The One Recipe To Rule Them All!

Suggested with love by Chef Dragana!

Since cooking is not one my strengths this simple 5 step recipe is the best I got

Directions

  1. Take out crock pot
  2. Take all items off your counter and throw them into the crock pot
  3. Take out your phone (hopefully it was not on the counter, if so look for it inside the pot) and open the Grubhub app (hint: it's the orange one)
  4. Pick one of your favorites from the top and reorder
  5. Wait 35-40 min for delicious food

Bonus step (optional): After one week anything in the crockpot you did not need over the past week can be tossed!
