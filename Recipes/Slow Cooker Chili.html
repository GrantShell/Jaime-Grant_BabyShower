Slow Cooker Chili

Suggested with love by Grandma Betty and Grandpa Steve!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 85% lean ground beef
  • 2 T chili powder
  • 2 T tomato paste
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 ½ t ground cumin
  • 1 15oz can kidney beans, rinsed
  • 1 15oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 14 ½ oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 ½ T soy sauce
  • 1 ½ t packed brown sugar
  • 1 t minced canned chipotle chile in adobo sauce
  • Salt and pepper

    Directions

    1. Lightly spray inside of slow cooker with vegetable oil spray.
    2. Microwave ground beef, chili powder, tomato paste, garlic, and cumin in bowl, stirring occasionally, until beef is no longer pink, about 5 mins.
    3. Transfer mixture to prepared slow cooker, breaking up any large pieces of meat with a wooden spoon.
    4. Stir in beans, tomato sauce, tomatoes and their juice, soy sauce sugar, chipotle, ½ t salt and ½ t of pepper. Cover and cook until beef is tender, 5-6 hours on low or 3-4 hours on high.
    5. Using large spoon, skim excess fat from surface of chili.
    6. Break up any remaining large pieces of beef with spoon.
    7. Season with salt and pepper to taste and serve.
