Chicken Taco Mix

Suggested with love by Whitney and Neil!

Ingredients

  • One rotisserie chicken, shredded (cooked and shredded chicken breast works just as well!)
  • One can cream of chicken
  • 1/2 packet taco seasoning 
  • one can chicken stock
  • one can black beans
  • one can corn
  • one can diced tomatoes
  • one can green chilies (sometimes I add a second because they are delicious)

Directions

  1. Toss shredded chicken, cream of chicken, taco seasoning and stock into a pot and bring to a slight bubble.  
  2. Place heat on low, add everything else and let cook for 20-30 minutes.
  3. Serve with desired toppings:  tortilla chips, sour cream, cheese and/or jalapeños!
